Evaluation

Our expert investigators evaluate job seekers and commercial and medical establishments using the highest international standards. In most cases our evaluations focus on criteria and conditions provided by the client. We provide advice and guidance using comprehensive summaries of our evaluations and any factors specific to any individual evaluation. Clients may rely entirely on our experts for thorough evaluations. We offer many evaluation programs to ensure a good fit between employers and employees or between government agencies and establishments in all sectors. Evaluation services enhance the quality of manpower, the readiness of enterprises, and the cost of human resources among many other factors. We offer individually customized programs to meet client needs from screening and evaluating job applicants and establishments to monitoring and assessing products and markets.
