Data Check

Data Check aims to review and audit all information and details contained in any kind of documents in adherence to relevant laws and regulations. We do not rely on stamps, seals, and official attestations on documents due to the ease with which they can be forged and attributed to formal sources. Data Check verifies the documents and information they contain directly with the issuing source to ensure that the source is legally authorised to issue such documents. The world is awash with false organizations and forged documents for the purpose illegitimate and illegal gain and Data Check verifies documents of behalf of its customers. Data Check’s primary category of document checks are for academic qualifications (diplomas), certificates of professional experience, professional licenses, followed by contracts, cheques from foreign banks, and a variety of other documents. We currently deal with more than 45,000 document issuers and this number is constantly growing. Contact us for more details.
